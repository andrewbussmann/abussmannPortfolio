Rubik Cubing

I started cubing in 6th grade and have been hooked ever since. My personal best time for a three by three is 16.32 seconds. I have a collection of about 30 different cubes including shapeshifters and bigger cubes than a standard Rubik Cube.

Diving

This year was my first year of diving. I am on the varsity Swim and Dive Team and have attended many competitions with my team. I placed third place in an eleven dive meet for JV Sections.

Mountain Biking

I am on the High School Mountain Biking Team and we go around the state to compete against other cities in the state.
